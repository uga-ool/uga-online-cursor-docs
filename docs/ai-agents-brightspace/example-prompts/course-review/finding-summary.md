Provide your complete, detailed analysis. Include: what specific course elements you examined, what evidence you found (cite module names, topic titles, quiz names, etc.), and how the evidence relates to the standard. This is the primary record of the review — do not condense or abbreviate. Use markdown for structure (## headings, - bullet lists, **bold**).
