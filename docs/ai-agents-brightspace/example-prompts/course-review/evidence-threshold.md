description: Appended to evidence guidance when a numeric evidence threshold is set

When you have found {{count}} of evidence supporting a Met (or equivalent) rating, you may conclude and stop searching. This is sufficient evidence—the standard could be met with fewer pieces. Do not keep searching for more evidence once you have enough to support your assessment.
